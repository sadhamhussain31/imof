Creative Kinder Garten Olympiad

Creative Kinder Garten Olympiads - 2022-23

CMOF being conducted in the states of India, UAE, KSA, Srilanka, Malaysia, Singapore, Nepal, Bangladesh.
Eligibility

Students studying Classes Nursery, Jr. KG, Sr.KG

Question Paper will be English Only.

Exam Schedule

Date of Examination: AUG- 10th , Sept,10th , Oct 10th ,Nov 10th  of 2022

Time: 10:30 A.M. to 12:00 Noon onwards.

Last Date For Receipt Of Applications:

As per the schedule before 7 working days

Exam Fee

Rs. 170 ( One Hundred & Seventy Rupees only)

Self Center And Courier Charges :

Rs.20/- can be deducted.

How To Apply

  • Please send a single consolidated Demand Draft for the exam fee of all the students appearing for the CKGO from your school.
  • We require a minimum of 200 students to allot self test centre.
  • The Teacher In-charge of the examination has to conduct the same according to schedule.
  • D.D. should be drawn in favor of M/s. CMOFEDU SOLUTION PVT. LTD, payable at Hyderabad.
  • For further details please send a mail (care@cmofeduworld.org) / write a letter for complete information brochure of CKGO-India.

Syllabus

CKGO will be based on States of India syllabi. Only 50% of the syllabus is considered for examination question papers.

  • All questions are in subjective type only.
  • Each class will have a distinct set of question papers.

Certificates

Students who get more than 75% of marks will be given Merit Certificates and for those who get more than 35% of marks will be given Participation Certificates.

Awards

  • First Ranker & Second Ranker in each class will be awarded with a Tablet +Memento + Cash prize of 10,000 (District Wide).
  • Third Ranker in each class will be awarded with a cash prize of Rs. 5000+ Memento. (District Wide).
  • Campus Wise: 1st & 2nd Ranker from class III & IV in each District will be awarded with CMOFS Talent & Olympiad exams Resource Book and classes V-XII will be awarded Exclusive Book Material worth Rs.999 + Certificate of Merit.
  • All the State top 3 rankers & District rankers in each class will be awarded with Cash Prizes of  Rs.20,000+ Certificate of Merit and next year free enrollment and DMIT -REPORT and Counseling FREE
  • We declare a School Topper from every School sponsoring a minimum of 100 students and he/she will be awarded with a Medal & Certificate of appreciation.
  • School Topper award will be declared for only those schools which are not in top 3 ranks and District ranks.

Institutional Awards

  • Best Performance Award – School
  • Five top performing schools in CKGO will be presented with Best Performance School award. – School of Academic Excellence Award. The Teacher in charge of the respective schools will be awarded with a Mobile Phone.

Best Participation Award - School

  • Will be presented to all the schools which forward more than 200 applications.
  • All the Principals & co-coordinating Teacher In-charges of the exam will be awarded a certificate of appreciation.

Note : Venue of the CKGO - INDIA will be respective schools only. There is no open center facility for above examination.
