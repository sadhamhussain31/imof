STATE OLYMPIAD

State Semmozhi Tamil Olympiad (SSTO)

State Semmozhi Tamil Olympiad (SSTO): Semmozhi Tamil Olympiad is our pride Olympiad we are the first organization who conduct Tamil Olympiad globally. The questions in these exams are in MCQ format which needs to be solved within 60 Min. SSTO Open to all the students of classes 1 to 10. Students can easily participate in these exams by filling in the registration form from the website. Once the student completes the registration students receive alerts and notifications for the exams. As these exams are conducted on both platforms. Online exam students can conveniently take the exam from their respective homes. if you had a laptop, desktop, or tablet with a webcam and live internet connection.

IMPORTANT NOTE: [Olympiads held in two levels]

All prizes & rewards are non-transferable & non-en cashable.

All the entries received will be the property of IMOF @ Chennai.

At least 100 students per school should be enrolled for the application forms to be accepted.

No limit for maximum students. Class-wise top three medals (Gold, Silver and Bronze medals) and awards will be given only if the student count is more than or equal to 20 per class per subject.

HOW TO PREPARE: The Olympiad is based on the syllabuses of Tamilnadu state boards academic syllabus. The students should learn the subject thoroughly to achieve excellence in these exams. Students should also try solving different questions based on the topics to check their knowledge solving several questions is an absolute
Date of Exam: Flexibility provided to schools to choose a date
Application to Exam: Typically, through schools as well as encourage individuals’ participation
Please send a single consolidated Demand Draft for the exam fee of all the students appearing for the SSTO from your school.
  • We require a minimum of 200 students to allot self-test canter.
  • The Teacher In-charge of the examination has to conduct the same according to schedule.
  • D.D. should be drawn in favour of M/s. IMOF EDU SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED payable at CHENNAI.
